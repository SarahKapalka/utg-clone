How To Apply

Please read instructions carefully before completing each step to avoid any mistake

STEP 1

Fill the form on the left hand side of the page and click on the the submit button as shown at the bottom of the page to create your account, upon subission, which you will be redirected to the login page.

STEP 2

Login with the email and the password you have entered in the form to proceed on with your application.

STEP 3

Upon starting your application process you will be redirected again to a page where you need to enter your pin code, please enter your fourteen digit pin code to unveil you the UTG online admission form.

4. MORE INFORMATION

Please make sure you fill in all the required fields from stage 1-9 and submit the form online. UTG will notify you both through SMS and Email to inform you about the status of your online application.
